Key Insights

Reliance Industries delivered a strong long-term CAGR of ~20% over nearly three decades.

2006 was the best-performing year, while 2008 was the worst, reflecting market cycles and financial crises.

Post-2016 price growth is supported by higher trading volume, indicating strong investor confidence.

Volatility remains moderate despite long-term growth, suggesting relative price stability.

Seasonal patterns are visible, with stronger performance in the second half of the year.
